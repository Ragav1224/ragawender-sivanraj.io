This is a java application that can trace assets of the university laboratory. Here the backend is MySQL. In this app Head of the departments only can assign the responsible officer. SLRC 2018

This is a robotic competition organized by the Electronics club of the University of Moratuwa, in 2018. We were preparing our team and I am the leader of our team. We couldn't win the place. But we tried our best and learned a lot. We build this robot within three sleepless nights for the competition. Here we build two Arduino robots that can communicate wirelessly. The main robot can follow the line, Detect color, Do some calculations, collect coins (Sri Lankan 2 rs coin which was covered by color sheets), and communicate wirelessly. The secondary robot can communicate wirelessly, indicate the number by which was sent by the main robot in binary format, and can move the obstacle on the left side or right side(The decision made using the number which was sent by the main robot). finally, the main robot should place the coins which were collected in an appropriate place.
